# AI and generated-content disclosure

BGOBS allows responsible use of generative tools. Parts of the repository have
been drafted, translated, reviewed, or refactored with assistance from tools
including OpenAI ChatGPT/Codex and GitHub Copilot. This includes documentation,
localization work, tests, review findings, and implementation changes.

Generated suggestions are not treated as an authority or as proof of
correctness. The maintainer reviews accepted changes, runs the applicable
checks, and remains responsible for licensing, security, and behavior. Git
history and file-level SPDX notices remain the authoritative provenance record.

Repository automation also generates lock files, build artifacts, website
output, checksums, and attestations. Generated artifacts are not preferred
source and should be reproduced from the corresponding source revision and
workflow whenever possible.

Contributors using AI assistance should follow the review and disclosure rules
in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md). In particular, do not submit output that
you do not understand or cannot lawfully license.
