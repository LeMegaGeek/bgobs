# BGOBS 0.3.15

- Start CaCam in ADB mode with an explicit frame rate instead of retaining an
  old override such as 60 FPS.
- Output the CaCam source on the OBS video canvas so its scene rectangle remains
  stable across Standard, HD, and UHD qualities.
- Document Low at 12 FPS as the smooth setting observed on the MI8 ADB test
  device.
